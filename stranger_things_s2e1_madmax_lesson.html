Episode story

Click underlined words for a Russian hint. The text is an original educational summary, not a transcript.

In October 1984, a group of young criminals escape from the police in Pittsburgh. One of them appears to create a powerful illusion. Her wrist carries the number 008, suggesting that Eleven was not the only child involved in secret experiments.

In Hawkins, Mike, Dustin, Lucas and Will spend time at the local arcade. They discover that a mysterious player called “MADMAX” has broken a record. The boys become curious and want to identify the new champion.

Will suddenly experiences another frightening event. For a moment, the familiar town disappears and he sees a dark version of Hawkins beneath a red storm. A gigantic shadow creature appears in the sky. Will returns to normal, but he is deeply shaken. Joyce takes him to the laboratory, where a doctor questions him. The doctor suggests that these episodes may be temporary memories, yet Will is not convinced.

Meanwhile, Hopper is asked to investigate a case involving damaged pumpkin fields. The plants are rotting from the inside, and the problem is spreading. Hopper initially treats it as an ordinary dispute, although the dead crops may be a warning sign.

At school, the boys finally meet Max, a confident new student who enjoys skateboarding and arcade games. Dustin and Lucas are immediately interested in her, while Mike remains distant. Max's older stepbrother Billy behaves aggressively and appears determined to control her.

Nancy still feels guilty about Barb. She and Steve visit Barb's parents, who still do not know the truth. The authorities continue to keep the events secret.

The final scene reveals another major secret: Eleven is alive. She is living in a hidden cabin with Hopper. He protects her, but she must remain indoors and follow strict rules. Their relationship is caring but tense, because Eleven wants greater freedom.

Stage 510 minutes

Grammar focus: predictions and possibility

I think…

Use it for a personal prediction or opinion.

I think Will is still connected to the Upside Down.

He / She / It may…

Use may + base verb for possibility.

Max may join the group.

It is likely that…

Use it when something seems probable.

It is likely that the dead fields are connected.

There is a possibility that…

Use it for a cautious, more formal prediction.

There is a possibility that Eleven will leave the cabin.

Choose the best prediction
